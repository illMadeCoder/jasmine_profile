About Me

Hello, I’m Jasmine! I have 4+ years of experience in childcare (2 years as a nanny, and 2 years in a daycare and UPK classroom). I’ve worked with kids aged 12 months to 8 years old. I have a passion for fostering problem solving skills and independence, encouraging healthy eating, and exploring the world around us. I’m inspired by Maria Montessori, and the Gentle Parenting philosophy. I pride myself on finding ways to make learning fun and engaging, and I’m definitely not afraid to be silly! Seeing the world through young and curious eyes brings me a lot of joy, and I think my genuine enthusiasm shines through in what I do.
